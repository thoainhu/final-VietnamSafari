PRESENTATION

Rainforest Fights

This entertaining and engaging presentation features a diverse range of rainforest animals. Be awed as they show off their dexterity by jumping and flying above.

Splash Safari

Our adorable performer is sure to leave a lasting impression, as well as get you thinking about how we can all do our part to save the ocean.

Lorikeet Meeting

Meet Benji and Buddy the golden parrots, Katie and George of the snow-white Japanese Spitz bird as well as Hope, from Singapore jungle — the oldest Lorikeet on the presentation.

Toucan Show

You will be able to watch the spectacular wingspans of Toucan, Hong Hoang, … or the adorable mischief of Otters and Civets – animals you will never see in a circus

Schedules

Catch our presentations to witness the amazing talents of our animals alongside their trainers. Our presentations play twice daily and each lasts for 15 to 20 minutes, weather permitting!

Wheelchair-friendly area

We have designated wheelchair zones at the Shaw Foundation Amphitheatre that can accommodate up to six and 15 wheelchairs respectively. So, even those with limited mobility will have a chance to catch the presentations!

Good to know

Arrive early

Arrive early before each presentation begins as seats are subject to availability, on a first-come-first-served basis.

Get splash-ready!

The Splash Safari presentation has designated splash zones. Occupy these seats only if you do not mind some wet and wild fun.

Turn off that flash

Kindly refrain from using flash photography during presentations as it may distract the animals.

Stay behind!

We usually have photography sessions at the end of the presentation. So, be sure to stick around for a snap with our animals!
